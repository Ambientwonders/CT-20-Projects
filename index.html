CT20 Creative Coding Projects

Conceptual projects exploring how systems maintain control over animal bodies through space, narrative, and time.

Project 1 – On Fairness, Movement, & The Marketing of Bodies

Asking the question "Is this a Fair Life?" through remix and a fading image reveal.

Project 2 – in documentation, a fracture

Interpreting “pattern” as something which occurs so often and repetitively that it can become "invisible" within the dominant experience of daily life. Move Cursor to Unlock Meaning.

Project 3 – The Body Clock

A clock that measures time through commodified animal bodies.

The Body Clock is conceptual. To approach statistical reality, it would need to add hundreds of thousands - likely millions - of bodies every minute.
